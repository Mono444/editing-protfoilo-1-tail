About Me

I'm Alex Mercer — a storyteller who speaks in cuts, transitions, and color grades.

With over 8 years in professional video editing, I've crafted cinematic experiences for YouTubers, global brands, independent filmmakers, and everything in between. My editing philosophy is simple: every frame should serve the story.

I specialize in cinematic color grading, dynamic pacing, and seamless visual effects that elevate raw footage into compelling narratives. Whether it's a 15-second reel or a 2-hour documentary, I bring the same level of passion and precision to every project.
